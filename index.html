About us

Personyfi allows users to send a bundle of personalized text messages simutaniously, creating a more effective way of communicating while saving you time.

The problem

Relaying the same text message on a large scale is cumbersome and slow

The solution

An app that offers a seamless, simple, and slick interface to deliver personalized text messages to a vast audience

Founders

Lucas and Anders met in University of Oregon's computer science department and have been building products since 2015.

Personyfi stemmed from wasting precious time over the holidays to send out personalized text messages to friends and loved ones. Personyfi fixes this problem through our slick and simple application interface. Keeping Google's Material Design in mind, we wanted to create a product that gives users the easiest platform to deliver their message powerfully. By utilizing asynchonous message passing, Personyfi blows expectations in texting performance.
